# Abuse and Copyright Policy

## Reporting

Reports may be submitted through `/abuse` without authentication.

Reports should include: - affected name; - description; - evidence; -
optional contact information.

## Available Remedies

Following governance approval the DAO may: - stop serving content; -
return HTTP 451 responses; - maintain block lists.

The DAO cannot: - remove NFTs; - remove IPFS content; - revoke
ownership.

## Copyright

wget.club does not operate a DMCA designated agent program.

Copyright complaints are handled through the standard governance
process.

## Counter Notices

Affected parties may submit responses through `/abuse`.

## Repeat Abuse

The DAO may maintain restrictions on names, wallets or patterns of
conduct associated with repeated abuse.
