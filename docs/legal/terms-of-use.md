# wget.club Terms of Use

## 1. Overview

wget.club is operated by wget.club DAO LLC, a Wyoming DAO LLC governed
exclusively through on-chain governance by tokenholders.

wget.club provides decentralized software distribution infrastructure in
which ERC-721 names on the Base blockchain resolve to mutable or
immutable IPFS content identifiers ("CIDs").

The service is designed primarily for software, scripts, binaries,
installers and executable code distribution.

Examples include:

-   `wget wget.club/<name>`
-   `curl -sSL wget.club/<name> | bash`

Users acknowledge that content obtained through wget.club is frequently
intended for direct execution.

## 2. Acceptance of Risk

Users acknowledge that software distributed through wget.club may:

-   execute arbitrary code;
-   modify operating systems;
-   install packages;
-   create persistent services;
-   access credentials or secrets;
-   transmit information externally;
-   perform privileged operations;
-   contain vulnerabilities or malicious behavior.

Users assume all risks associated with downloading, compiling,
installing or executing content.

## 3. Mutable Releases

Unless explicitly frozen, NFT owners may update the CID associated with
a name at any time.

A previously trusted name may later resolve to different content.

Users are strongly encouraged to: - verify CIDs; - inspect source
code; - verify signatures; - prefer frozen names for production
deployments; - retain known-good versions.

## 4. Ownership and Property Rights

Names are ERC-721 assets existing independently of wget.club
infrastructure.

wget.club DAO LLC cannot: - revoke ownership; - seize names; - transfer
ownership; - destroy NFTs; - remove names from the blockchain.

Ownership of a name does not guarantee continued distribution through
wget.club.

## 5. Fees

The protocol may charge fees for: - registration; - updates; -
governance approved services.

The protocol receives a 5% royalty on supported secondary sales.

Payments are final and non-refundable except where applicable law
requires otherwise.

## 6. Prohibited Content

The protocol may not be used for: - malware; - ransomware; - spyware; -
phishing; - credential theft; - sanctions violations; - intellectual
property infringement; - unlawful content; - fraud.

## 7. Enforcement

The DAO may decide to stop distributing content through wget.club
infrastructure.

Available actions include: - block lists; - HTTP 451 responses; -
refusal of future distribution.

The DAO cannot remove NFTs or remove content from IPFS.

## 8. Governance

All governance actions are performed exclusively by tokenholders.

No emergency council, administrator or multisig possesses authority to
override governance decisions.
